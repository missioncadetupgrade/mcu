1. What is Mission Cadet Upgrade? +
Mission Cadet Upgrade is an initiative started by the final-year NCC cadets of Kongunadu Arts and Science College. It focuses on improving communication, leadership, current affairs awareness, and career preparation.
2. Who can join Mission Cadet Upgrade? +
Any NCC cadet from any wing or year can join and participate in the programs.
3. What activities are conducted? +
Workshops, seminars, quizzes, career guidance, mock tests, group studies, and motivational sessions.
4. Which exams are covered? +
UPSC, TNPSC, AFCAT, CDS, INET, SSC, and other competitive exams.
5. Is there a membership fee? +
No. Mission Cadet Upgrade is completely free for all cadets.
6. How can I participate? +
Through announcements from the NCC unit or by following official social media pages.
7. Who organizes the sessions? +
Final-year cadets organize the programs with support from NCC officers and faculty advisors.
8. Benefits of joining? +
Improved confidence, communication, leadership skills, exam support, teamwork, and motivation.
9. Do you invite external trainers? +
Yes, guest speakers, alumni, and professionals are invited for special sessions.
10. How to contact Mission Cadet Upgrade? +
Through the NCC office, social media pages, or senior cadet coordinators.
