1. What is Mission Cadet Upgrade?
Mission Cadet Upgrade is an initiative started by the final-year NCC cadets...
2. Who can join Mission Cadet Upgrade?
All NCC cadets can join regardless of year or wing.
3. Activities conducted?
Workshops, seminars, quizzes, exam guidance, etc.
4. Exams helpful for?
UPSC, TNPSC, AFCAT, CDS, INET, SSC, etc.
5. Membership fee?
No fee. It's volunteer-based.
6. How to participate?
Follow announcements or MCU social media.
7. Who organizes it?
Final-year cadets + NCC officers.
8. Benefits?
Leadership, communication, confidence, exam support.
9. Guest speakers?
Yes, alumni + professionals join sessions.
10. Contact MCU?
Through NCC office or MCU social media.
