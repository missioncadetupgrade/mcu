Frequently Asked Questions

Discipline • Leadership • Service to the Nation

1. What is Mission Cadet Upgrade? +
Mission Cadet Upgrade is a disciplined NCC-led initiative focused on leadership, communication, current affairs, and officer-oriented career preparation.
2. Who can join this mission? +
Any NCC cadet from any wing or academic year can participate.
3. What activities are conducted? +
Leadership workshops, defence awareness sessions, mock tests, seminars, quizzes, and career guidance.
4. Which exams are covered? +
NDA, CDS, AFCAT, INET, UPSC, TNPSC, SSC, and other competitive exams.
5. Is there any fee? +
No. This mission is completely free for all cadets.
6. Who conducts the sessions? +
Senior NCC cadets, officers, faculty advisors, alumni, and guest experts.
7. What are the benefits? +
Improved confidence, leadership mindset, teamwork, discipline, and exam readiness.
8. How can I contact the team? +
Through NCC unit announcements, senior cadets, or official communication channels.
