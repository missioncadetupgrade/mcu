Mission Cadet Upgrade

Who We Are

Mission Cadet Upgrade is a student-driven association formed by final-year NCC cadets. Our mission is to nurture cadets in leadership, communication, and confidence.

Our Vision

To create disciplined and career-ready cadets who embody leadership and excellence.

Our Mission

  • Enhance personality development
  • Create career awareness
  • Promote current affairs knowledge
  • Guide for competitive exams
  • Encourage teamwork and mentorship

Our Story

Started by final-year NCC seniors, the mission evolved into a structured leadership platform.

Our Motto

Empowering Cadets for a Better Tomorrow.
