Who We Are

Mission Cadet Upgrade is a student-driven association formed by the final-year NCC cadets of Kongunadu Arts and Science College. Our mission is to nurture and guide our fellow cadets in developing essential life skills, improving communication, and building confidence to excel in every field they pursue.

We believe that being an NCC cadet goes beyond discipline and drills — it’s about becoming a well-rounded individual ready to serve the nation with knowledge, leadership, and integrity.

Our Vision

To create a community of confident, disciplined, and career-ready cadets who excel in both personal and professional spheres, embodying the values of leadership, service, and excellence.

Our Mission

  • Enhance personality development and communication skills.
  • Create awareness about career opportunities.
  • Promote current affairs and intellectual discussions.
  • Guide cadets for UPSC, TNPSC, AFCAT, CDS, INET, SSC, etc.
  • Motivate cadets through mentorship and teamwork.

Our Story

Founded by final-year NCC seniors, Mission Cadet Upgrade started as peer-to-peer learning sessions and evolved into a structured leadership platform.

Today, it empowers cadets to think beyond the uniform — to lead, serve, and succeed.

Our Motto

“Empowering Cadets for a Better Tomorrow.”
